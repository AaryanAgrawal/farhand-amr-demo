# Livox Mid-360 LiDAR Sensor - Technical Specifications

## Core Performance
- **Laser Wavelength:** 905 nm
- **Detection Range:** 40m @ 10% reflectivity, 70m @ 80% reflectivity (at 100 klx)
- **Close Proximity Blind Zone:** 0.1m
- **Point Rate:** 200,000 points/s (first return)
- **Frame Rate:** 10 Hz (typical)

## Accuracy & Precision
- **Range Precision (1σ):** ≤ 2 cm at 10m, ≤ 3 cm at 0.2m
- **Angular Precision (1σ):** < 0.15°
- **False Alarm Rate:** < 0.01% at 100 klx

## Field of View
- **Horizontal FOV:** 360°
- **Vertical FOV:** -7° to 52°

## Physical
- **Dimensions:** 65 x 65 x 60 mm
- **Weight:** 265g
- **IP Rating:** IP67

## Power & Environment
- **Average Power:** 6.5W
- **Supply Voltage:** 9-27V DC
- **Operating Temp:** -20°C to 55°C (-4°F to 131°F)
- **Peak Power (self-heating):** 14W in cold conditions

## Connectivity
- **Data Port:** 100BASE-TX Ethernet
- **Synchronization:** IEEE 1588-2008 (PTPv2), GPS
- **Built-in IMU:** ICM40609

## Safety
- **Laser Class:** Class 1 (IEC60825-1:2014) - Eye Safe
- **Anti-Interference:** Supported

## Network Defaults
- **LiDAR IP:** 192.168.1.1xx (configurable)
- **Host IP:** 192.168.1.50 (configurable)
- **Command Port:** 56100
- **Data Port:** 56200
- **IMU Port:** 56300
